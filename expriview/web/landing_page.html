Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Expriview and its features.

How does Expriview analyze emotions during interviews?

Expriview uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze facial expressions to provide real-time emotional insights.

What are the benefits of using Expriview in interviews?

Expriview helps provide deeper emotional understanding by analyzing facial expressions, improving the quality of feedback during interviews and enhancing communication dynamics.

How accurate is Expriview in detecting emotions?

Expriview utilizes sophisticated AI to analyze facial expressions. While accuracy can vary depending on context, lighting, and individual differences, it provides reliable emotional insights for most situations.

How can I interpret the emotional insights provided by Expriview?

Expriview offers a visual representation of your emotional responses, showing the intensity and nature of emotions detected throughout the interview. This data helps you understand how emotions influence communication.

Can Expriview be used for group interviews?

Expriview is primarily designed for one-on-one interviews, but it can be adapted for group settings. However, separate analysis for each participant may be required for optimal results.

Does Expriview store my facial expression data?

Your facial expression data is securely processed and stored only for the purpose of analysis. Expriview ensures that your personal information and emotional insights are kept private and confidential.
